Accessible

Accessible is a review service that lists user-based accessibility recommendations within the local area for people who care about making the world a more accessible place.

Background

The task of the project was to redesign yelp with an individual focus on a specific audience or use-case. I had the option to completely redesign Yelp and act as though it didnâ€™t exist, or I could create something that could co-exist with it today.

Implementation

After looking at the information that I gathered from my research I needed to start thinking about the structure of the app and what features needed to be added to create something outstanding.

2 Review Systems
Accessibility ratings are the number one priority on the app, however while browsing around where to go it would also be helpful to know the general quality of the venue. Also including a system for rating the quality of a place would directly compete with yelp. I realized a problem of creating a separate space solely for people with disabilities and taking them out of the community of yelp. My solution was to integrate yelp into the app in a minor way and have it quickly link externally to the yelp page.
User Filters
User filters allow for the user to choose what accessibility features to be shown on a review page. This allows for people with different needs to see only what they need to versus having all of the information shown.
